About the author

Writing at the edge of ideas.

I'm Shree — a writer, designer, and lifelong learner based in San Francisco. I started Inkwell in 2021 as a place to think out loud about the things that matter most to me.

My writing lives at the intersection of technology and the human experience: how we create, how we think, and what it means to live and work with intention in a fast-moving world.

I believe good writing is a form of respect — for the reader's time, intelligence, and curiosity. That belief shapes everything published here.

The Story So Far
